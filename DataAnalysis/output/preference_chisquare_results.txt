Chi-square goodness-of-fit test for UI preference
=======================================================

Observed counts:
  C0 (Static UI): 7
  C1 (Dynamic UI (Left Movement)): 4
  C2 (Dynamic UI (Upward Movement)): 9

Expected counts under H0 (equal preference):
  C0 (Static UI): 6.667
  C1 (Dynamic UI (Left Movement)): 6.667
  C2 (Dynamic UI (Upward Movement)): 6.667

Total N = 20
Chi-square statistic = 1.9000
Degrees of freedom = 2
p-value = 0.386741

Result: Fail to reject H0 at alpha = 0.05.
The preference distribution does not significantly differ from equal preference.

Note:
This test evaluates whether the three preference categories are equally likely overall.
It does not directly test whether C2 is significantly preferred over both C0 and C1 individually.
